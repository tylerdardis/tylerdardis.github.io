Hi, I'm Tyler! I'm a graduate of Minnesota State University, Mankato with a degree in Professional Geography and a certificate in GISc. I have several years of professional experience in the environmental field as a client success manager. I have assisted hundreds of clients with their environmental data questions, concerns, and issues; garnering myself expert level knowledge of site assessment materials. I also have experience being a direct researcher for these same materials, utilizing GIS to geo-reference and pull historical aerials, topographic maps, and more. I am a team player that has worked on and managed teams to provide exceptional data that has been quality assured and delivered in a swift and professional manner.
